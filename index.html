About Us

Rutgers Competitive Programming aims to train the official Rutgers teams to programming competitions like the prestigious ACM-ICPC, in addition to online contests like Codeforces, HackerRank, and LeetCode. In weekly meetings, members learn and practice problem-solving techniques covering algorithmic and mathematical concepts like dynamic programming, graph/tree theory, geometry and number theory, to name a few. RUCP will stretch your problem solving skills and prepare you for coding interviews at the top tech companies. The only prerequisite is basic knowledge of coding (in any language), and a charged laptop!
